# Bundled translation editions — sources & licensing

These offline editions are generated by `Scripts/build_translations.py` from the
[quran.com](https://quran.com) v4 API and shipped so non-English readers work offline with
correct attribution (no more English text shown under a non-English translator's credit).

| Code | Edition | quran.com id | Notes on rights |
|------|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| `ur` | Fatah Muhammad Jalandhari | 234 | Translator d. 1954 → **public domain** (life + 70). Chosen over Maududi/Tafheem (id 97), which is copyrighted, specifically so it can be shipped. |
| `id` | Kementerian Agama Republik Indonesia | 33 | Official edition of the Indonesian Ministry of Religious Affairs; in wide free distribution. |
| `tr` | Diyanet İşleri | 77 | Official edition of the Turkish Presidency of Religious Affairs (Diyanet); in wide free distribution. |

**Before shipping to the App Store, confirm redistribution terms** for the Indonesian (33)
and Turkish (77) government editions for your distribution. If either is a concern, swap it
for a public-domain or explicitly-licensed edition: change the resource id + credit in
`EDITIONS` (Scripts/build_translations.py) and in `TranslationLanguage` (AppModels.swift),
then re-run the script. The Urdu edition (Jalandhari) is public domain and safe as-is.

English ships inside `FullQuran.json` (Saheeh International). Spanish and French are not
bundled — they load live from quran.com, and fall back to English (clearly labelled) offline.
